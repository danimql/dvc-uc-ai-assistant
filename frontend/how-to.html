How to use this bot

  1. Open the Chat tab.
  2. Ask about one or more campuses (UCB / UCD / UCSD).
  3. Optionally say things like “required only”, “cs only”, or list courses you’ve finished (e.g., COMSC-110, MATH-192).
  4. Copy results to your notes or share with your counselor.

Great example prompts

  • major preparation only for UC Berkeley CS and UC Davis
  • show general education only for UC San Diego
  • cs only; completed COMSC-110 and COMSC-200; what’s left for UCD?

The bot parses your question, detects campuses, filters the DVC → UC mappings, and returns a clean list. Data sources live in /agreements_25-26 and /data inside the repo.
